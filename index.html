Project Summary:


We have generated a database containing names of major league baseball players and colleges that the players may have attended. Data contained in .csv files were downloaded from three websites as listed below. Tools used in the project included pandas (Jupyter Notebook) and MySQL. Final database included the players names and the name and location of the college or university they attended, if applicable.



Data Sources:


Our project used the following five csv files, all of which are saved in the project /Resources/ folder. The csv files were downloaded from the following three sources.


https://www.kaggle.com/seanlahman/the-history-of-baseball

- player.csv - lists major league baseball players and biographical info (not including college)

- player_college.csv - listing id of college attended for major league baseball players

- college.csv - listing college full name based on college id


https://www.kaggle.com/flyingwombat/us-news-and-world-reports-college-data

- College.csv - listing US Colleges and academic statistics such as graduation rate, number of applications, etc. from academic year 1995. The original csv file included data for over 700 colleges, including enrollment rates and total costs.

 

https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/college-scorecard

- 2016 college-scoreboard.csv - listing of over 7000 U.S. colleges and universities and included over a 100 statistics sponsored by the Department of Education. The original download for the website included csv files from school years 1996/97 through 2016/17. We used the csv file from the 2016/2017 school year. Most of the statistics were not in clear in meaning, and so the only fields used from this file were the college name, city, state, and zip code.
